# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 11, 2026_

## 1. What We Collect

**Account data:** When you sign in (via GitHub OAuth or email), we store your username, avatar URL, and email address.

**Worker data:** Code you deploy, environment variables, and bindings configuration.

**Usage data:** Request logs (URL, status code, timing) for your Workers. Logs are retained for a limited period and are only visible to you.

**Analytics:** We use Google Analytics on this website to track page views and basic visitor metrics.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- To operate the Service and run your Workers
- To enforce our [Terms of Service](/terms-of-service)
- To investigate abuse and security incidents
- To improve the Service

We do not sell your data.

## 3. What We Share

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except:

- When required by law
- With infrastructure providers necessary to operate the Service (hosting, DNS)

## 4. Data Retention

- **Account data** is retained until you delete your account
- **Worker code** is retained until you delete the worker or your account

## 5. Your Rights

You can:

- Export your worker code at any time via the CLI or dashboard
- Delete your workers and their associated data
- Delete your account and all associated data

## 6. Cookies

This website uses cookies for:

- **Authentication** (session cookies when signed in)
- **Analytics** (Google Analytics cookies on the documentation site)

No cookies are set by your deployed Workers unless your code explicitly sets them.

## 7. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13.

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

## 9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach out via [GitHub](https://github.com/openworkers) or [Telegram](https://t.me/openworkers).
